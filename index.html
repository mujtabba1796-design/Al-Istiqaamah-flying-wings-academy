📖 Our Mission

Al‑Istiqaamah strives to develop an enriched learning environment that promotes academic achievement, leadership and Islamic values, nurturing learners who live by the Qur’an and the Sunnah while contributing positively to society.

✨ Our Vision

To cultivate a crystal community of believers and learners who embody faith, knowledge, and inspiration — becoming beacons of guidance, excellence, and service to humanity.

Our Holistic Programmes

Tailored tracks that integrate Islamic knowledge, Qur’anic memorization, and academic excellence.

Regular Islamiyyah (RI)

To enrich minds, souls, and bodies with authentic Qur’anic and Prophetic Knowledge.

Timing: 4:00pm - 5:45pm (Mon - Wed)

Age/Gender: From 3+ / All

Subjects: Qur’an, Hadith, Fiqh, Tawhid, Seerah

Regular Memorization (RM)

Foster a joyful discipline of Qur’anic memorization within a nurturing environment.

Timing: 8:00am - 4:00pm (Sat & Sun)

Age/Gender: From 3+ / All

Focus: Qur’an, Tahdid, Tajweed

Intensive Memorization (IM)

Develop confident leaders through mastery of Qur’an and authentic Islamic teachings.

Timing: 8:00am - 4:00pm (Sat & Sun, excl. Fri)

Age/Gender: From 5+ / specialised track

Subjects: Qur’an, Hadith, Seerah, Sciences of Qur’an
